### Just Nerf
##General
Most country or planet modifiers have been heavily nerfed by a factor 2 minimum from ANY sources
(Mostly Research, Buildings, Edicts)

## Starbases and Orbitals rings modules
Cost and upkeep twice as much, provide half as much and now have a 0.5 dark matter upkeep

## Districts
Ecumonopolies and utopian ecu districts now have a corresponding rare ressource upkeep and provide less housing and amnities (max 2/3 to min 1/6 less)
Habitats districts now have higher energy upkeep up to x10 or have been introduced in some cases

## Building
Planet modifiers building are now limited to only one per planet
Added a rare ressource cost and upkeep
Most building cost more or less depending if it contains a planet modifier
Build time and basic ressource upkeep are overall increased

# Basic ressource buildings
Now produces ressources and jobs are now all aligned to FE buildings (except jobs for modifiers buildings)

#Advanced ressource building (motes, exotic, crystals, zro, living metal, nanites)
Now limited to only one per planet
Doesn't produce ressource anymore
Change industrialist jobs to produce the advanced ressource in exchange of an increased upkeep (the same as base Stellaris)
Minerals cost increase, introducing an energy cost, no change to upkeep
No rare ressource upkeep (as original mod)

# Advanced ressource building for dark matter
Same as above but now is dedicated for generator and industries districts with an high energy upkeep for both the building, technicians and industries jobs

## Decisions / Deposits
Major decisions now cost additional alloys
Utopian infrastructure max district 5 -> 3

Utopian infrastructure for Gestalts only :
Unity jobs are fixed to 400 instead of 10 (probably a bug in original mod)
Housing and amnities has been reduced by 1/5

## Terraforming
Terraforming out of molten and toxic world cost 4 times more

## Jobs
Job ressource production and upkeep are reduced by 1/3
